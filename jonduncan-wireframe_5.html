JON
DUNCAN
Motion Designer · Music Industry · London, UK

Specialist motion designer for the music industry with over a decade of experience creating lyric videos, live visuals, and promotional content for some of the world's biggest artists.

Worked with U2, Coldplay, Billie Eilish, Green Day, BTS, Madonna, Eels and many more — through agencies including Fat and Superglow.

Every project is built around the music. The type moves because the song demands it. The colour palette is pulled from the record. The edit breathes with the track.
