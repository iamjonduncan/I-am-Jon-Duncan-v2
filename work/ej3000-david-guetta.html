EJ3000 / David Guetta

Client EJ3000 / David Guetta
Type Lyric Video
Year 2023
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

EJ3000 is a new artist project built around anonymity: masked, hooded, and faceless. The brief was open, with raw footage of the artist dancing in an office as the only starting point.

I colour-graded and re-edited the footage to match the track's energy, with colour shifts tied to the audio. The figure was duplicated, mirrored, multiplied and pushed to near-abstraction across a strictly black, white and acid green palette. Alongside the footage, a suite of kinetic typography treatments runs throughout: the name "David Guetta" tiled, scrolling, warped and stacked at every scale, from full-bleed letters filling the frame to dense repeating grids of text. The two strands cut between each other to keep the pace up, with the typography, dancing and glitches becoming increasingly intense as the track builds.
