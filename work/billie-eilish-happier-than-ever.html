Billie Eilish / Happier Than Ever

Client Billie Eilish
Type Album Promo
Year 2022
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Billie Eilish is one of the biggest artists of her generation, with Happier Than Ever debuting at number one in over 20 countries.

We created a pair of 15-second vertical pre-order and out now assets to promote the album's exclusive vinyl formats across retail partners including Urban Outfitters, indie stores, and the Billie Eilish store. The treatment stays faithful to the album's aesthetic: a warm sand palette, the campaign's signature script typography, and an analogue vintage border with subtle grain and blur to give the whole piece a soft, tactile feel. Each frame cycles through the different coloured vinyl variants, with sold-out badges applied where relevant, before resolving on the release call to action.
