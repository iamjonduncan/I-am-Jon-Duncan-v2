Green Day / DSP

Client Green Day
Type DSP
Year 2025
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Green Day are one of the most influential punk rock bands of all time, with a catalogue spanning four decades and multiple generations of fans.

I created animated DSP assets for three titles in their back catalogue.

For Insomniac, I pulled apart the dense collage artwork by Winston Smith, the artist best known for his work with Dead Kennedys, separating out individual characters to animate independently: a man playing violin with an X-ray visible through his chest, fire building in the background, a woman playing guitar while holding a gun, and a monkey in a top hat balanced on a circus ball.

For Nimrod, I separated the layers of the cover to create movement in the background, applied a faux zoom across the portrait subjects, and built a looping animation of the iconic Nimrod sticker.

The Green Day Collection, I rebuilt the cover character as a fully animatable rig, brought in rolling clouds and an animated lightning strike that blows the figure off his feet, with the Green Day logo spray painted on and off screen.
