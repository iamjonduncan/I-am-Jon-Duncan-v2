Fatboy Slim / Everybody Needs A 303 (Harry Romero Remix)

Client Fatboy Slim
Type Visualiser
Year 2022
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Fatboy Slim is Norman Cook, one of the architects of big beat and one of the world's first superstar DJs. "Everybody Needs A 303" was his debut single, released in 1996.

The original video, directed by Ron Kurtz on a budget of £75, features a man standing in profile for the entire video, ending with "WHY MAKE VIDEOS" written on his forehead in lipstick. That provocation became the brief.

Working from the Harry Romero remix, I took that near-static footage and pushed it as far as it could go: fast cuts, mirrored faces, warping text, and distortion and colour effects driven by the audio itself in real time. A visualiser built entirely from the spirit of a video that never wanted to exist.
