Korn and Evanescence

Client Korn / Evanescence
Type Promo
Year 2022
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Korn and Evanescence are two of rock's most iconic acts, co-headlining their first tour together since 2007 across 18 US cities in summer 2022.

I created the tour announcement assets: a slow, cinematic build opening on a lone figure standing under a night sky, the moon drifting into frame as audio fragments from Korn's "Blind" and Evanescence's "Bring Me to Life" emerge from the atmosphere. The Korn logo etches itself into the surface of the moon while stars form the Evanescence logo overhead, before the whole scene explodes into pieces. All sound design and original music was composed for the piece, which was delivered across multiple aspect ratios for social and digital use.
