Kylie / Disco TV Ident

Client Kylie Minogue
Type Video Ident
Year 2020
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Disco TV was Kylie Minogue's 24/7 YouTube channel launched alongside her album Disco RETROPOP, playing her classic videos back to back with exclusive content.

I created a channel ident to run between videos, taking cues from classic MTV stings. A drab vintage TV flickers through glimpses of Kylie's hits before exploding into colour and energy as the Disco TV logo animates in. The sparkles were sourced directly from a dress Kylie wore on tour, grounding the piece in her world. The project also included animated DSP assets and YouTube visualisers for all 16 album tracks, plus a set of visualiser loops used as virtual backgrounds during her promotional interview campaign.
