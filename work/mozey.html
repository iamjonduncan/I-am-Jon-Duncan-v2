Mozey / LIVE VISUALS

Client Mozey
Type Live Visuals
Year 2026
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Mozey is a drum and bass DJ known for his tongue-in-cheek live shows. For his December set I created a suite of looping visual backgrounds to run throughout the hour-long performance.

The brief was humour in space, leaning deliberately into lo-fi, cheap early-2000s VFX as a comedic reference rather than something to fix. Working from the artist's green screen footage, the loops include: Mozey riding a cosmic dolphin and a rocket through space; his face mapped onto a rotating planet; a Darth Vader dating video with VHS overlays and neon hearts; a disco Jedi dance routine; a space cowboy in a sequin hat; walls of tumbling duplicated heads; and the artist launching through a Rick and Morty-style green slime portal in an orange NASA suit.
