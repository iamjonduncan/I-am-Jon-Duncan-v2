David Puentez x Malou / Talking Hands

Client David Puentez x Malou
Type Lyric Video
Year 2026
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

David Puentez is a German DJ and producer with a string of international club releases. Malou is a Swedish singer-songwriter known for her intimate vocal style.

The lyric video for Talking Hands takes its lead directly from the single artwork: a strictly monochrome world of shifting grain, soft light blooms and deep shadow. Heavy film grain runs throughout, with the background constantly breathing between black and white through deliberate inversions. Abstracted hand footage cuts through the texture at intervals, blurred and pushed to near abstraction, with the movement of the hands echoing the intimacy of the track's title. Lyrics appear in a spaced, understated typeface, using difference blending to embed the text into the texture rather than sitting on top of it, flickering between black and white depending on the surface beneath. Words scatter and disintegrate in and out, with the pacing of both the lyrics and the hand sequences adjusted throughout to sustain momentum and keep the edit alive.
