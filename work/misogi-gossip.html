Misogi / Gossip

Client Misogi
Type Lyric Video
Year 2022
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

MISOGI is a London-based producer and musician whose work sits at the intersection of hyperpop, indietronica and emo. Escape Artist leans into the ethos of early internet culture and a nostalgia for the digital iconography of the early 2000s.

Gossip was one of the album's lead singles. For the lyric video, the source material was candid footage shot during a photoshoot on three intentionally lo-fi formats: a DSLR, an iPhone and a Nintendo DS. Working with Misogi to develop the visual treatment, I processed and graded the footage into an intense CRT and glitch aesthetic, shifting colour temperature and scanline texture across each pass to create a suite of treatments that feel unstable and saturated. The footage was then edited together into a single continuous piece, with lyrics kept deliberately subtle and understated so the visuals remain the primary experience.
