The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

Tedeschi Trucks Band are a Grammy Award-winning 12-piece ensemble Live Nation Newsroom led by Susan Tedeschi and Derek Trucks, known for world-class musicianship and a live show that moves effortlessly through blues, soul, rock, and beyond.

Future Soul is their sixth studio album, released March 2026 via Fantasy Records. The cover artwork, depicting Susan and Derek as superheroes, was created by illustrator Marc Sasso, whose credits include cover art for Mötley Crüe, Dio and Judas Priest.

For the album's digital billboard campaign, we created a 10-second animated OOH asset designed to feel like the artwork was physically breaking out of the screen. The piece opens full-bleed before pulling back to reveal a framed layout, with the artist name and album title styled as neon signage, flickering in with sparks. The album's imagery then comes to life: UFOs streak in and hover, a robot lumbers forward and reaches its hand past the frame's edge, and the whole thing ends with a laser blast that cracks and glitches the screen.
