The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

"Wildpeace" is a poem by the late Israeli poet Yehuda Amichai, originally published in 1971, voiced here by Nigerian artist Adeola of Les Amazones d'Afrique, with music by U2 and producer Jacknife Lee. The Jerusalem Post It appears on the Days of Ash EP alongside the band's political song cycle addressing conflict and human cost.

The lyric video takes a deliberately restrained approach: a single zine page, gently scrolling, with words typing on as if being written in real time. Redacted paint blocks appear behind selected words for emphasis. Around the edges of the frame, flowers and plants slowly fade in with an inky softness, rendered in a light palette that references the watercolour paintings of Brian Eno. The visual supports the poem without competing with it.
