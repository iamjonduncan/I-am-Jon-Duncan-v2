U2 / American Obituary

Client U2 / Full Stop Management

The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

U2 are one of the biggest rock bands in history, with a catalogue spanning five decades and a reputation for music that confronts political and social realities head-on.

American Obituary, from their EP Days of Ash, was written in response to the fatal shooting of Renée Good, an unarmed civilian killed by a U.S. ICE officer in Minnesota. The track reached the top 10 in America.

The lyric video draws on the aesthetic of U2's own Propaganda zine: hand-printed textures, stamped and handwritten type, halftone-treated footage, and zine-style transitions that give the piece a raw, collapsed feel.

The chorus centres on a Statue of Liberty wrapped in tightening barbed wire, an image that builds and recurs across the edit as both motif and metaphor.
