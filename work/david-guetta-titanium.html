David Guetta ft. Sia / Titanium

Client David Guetta
Type Lyric Video
Year 2022
The Brief
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

David Guetta is one of the world's most streamed electronic artists, and "Titanium" featuring Sia remains one of his most iconic tracks. The Future Rave Remix, produced with MORTEN, was released in 2021 to mark a decade since the original.

I created the lyric video for the remix, building a high-energy visual world to match the track's hard-hitting rave sound. Bold, heavily stylised typography fills the frame across shifting gradient backgrounds, with lyrics treated differently across each section: stacked and echoing for the big moments, paired with cutout black-and-white imagery tied directly to the words. Missiles for "you shoot me down", a jet fighter for "ricochet", a wireframe figure for the "I am titanium" hook. Glitch effects and digital noise run throughout, keeping the energy at club level from start to finish.
